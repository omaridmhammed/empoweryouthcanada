About Empower Youth Canada

Founded in 2025, we believe that when young people are given the right tools, guidance, and opportunities, they can create lasting change in their own lives and in the world around them.

Our Story

2025

The Beginning

Empower Youth Canada was founded in Ottawa, Ontario by a small group of community leaders and youth advocates who recognized the growing need for equitable access to education, mentorship, and career opportunities‚Äîespecially for marginalized and underrepresented youth.

Rapid Growth

What started as a local initiative quickly grew into a nationwide movement. Within months, our programs began reaching youth across Canada through virtual training, community partnerships, and grassroots projects.

Building Partnerships

We built strong partnerships with schools, nonprofits, and industry leaders, enabling us to deliver programs that equip youth with leadership skills, digital literacy, sports, arts, mental health support, and pathways to meaningful employment.

Today

Empower Youth Canada stands as a bridge between potential and opportunity, ensuring that no young person is left behind‚Äîregardless of their background, circumstances, or location. Our story is still being written, and it's being shaped by every young person we empower.
